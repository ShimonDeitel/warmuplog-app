Privacy Policy

Warm-Up Log is an offline-first app. All data you enter (entries, dates, notes, and any values you type) is stored locally on your device in the app's own storage. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data with us or with third parties. There is no analytics, tracking, or advertising SDK in this app.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment information.

If you delete the app, your local data is deleted along with it. There is no cloud backup or account system.
